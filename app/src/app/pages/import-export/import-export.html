Trade Categories

Types of Import & Export We Handle

Dubai's trade economy spans a wide range of goods. Here's where we operate, and the categories that move through the UAE every day.

Metals & Aluminium

One of the UAE's leading exports. We manage bulk aluminium and metal shipments to key markets including India, Japan and South Korea.

Machinery & Equipment

Industrial and commercial machinery represent one of the UAE's largest import categories, sourced from global manufacturing hubs.

Petroleum & Chemicals

Crude oil, refined petroleum and industrial chemicals remain core export commodities moving through UAE ports.

Textiles & Fabrics

Cotton, fabrics and finished textiles flow heavily between the UAE and South Asian markets, particularly India and Pakistan.

Food & Consumables

Food products, dates and agricultural goods make up a significant share of both UAE imports and outbound re-exports.

Precious Metals & Gems

Gold, jewelry and precious stones represent one of the UAE's most significant trade categories by value on both sides.
